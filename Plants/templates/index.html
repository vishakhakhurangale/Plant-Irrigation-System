ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Plant Health

We have created a system which gives the current status of the plant in the farm.Indiana Farm sensors measure soil, plant and environmental variables to accurately measure water stress.Our advanced predicitive algorithms deliver real time irrigation access.Our project is to monitor the plant even from a far away distance where the actuators help in releaing the water in whenever there is a deficiency in the water level.This is done with the support of micro controllers and sensors.The sensors we used are soil moisture, temperature, humidity, rainfall.These values are compared with plant requirements which results in the activation of the actuators and hence help in irrigation of the plant.

Plant profile







The plant values are kept for the sake of assessment of the plant growth.Graphs are made and compared in the dashboard after logging in.The user can add a plant at any time he wants in his account and can know about the plant health wherver the user is.This project also helps the user know about the locality of the plant and which of the country it is suitable for the growth of the plant. The google maps and the latitude and longitude of the pkant adds an advantage for the user.
